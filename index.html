Setup Guide

  1. 01
    Enable Bookmarks Bar:
    If your bookmarks bar is currently hidden, enable it using Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (macOS).
  2. 02
    Drag & Drop:
    Click and hold the green "Xtrackgs Tool" button and drag it up into your browser’s bookmark area.
  3. 03
    Surf & Track:
    Go to any supported trading console (e.g. Axiom terminal). Click the bookmark in your bar. A beautiful hub will overlay on top, letting you sync raw Twitter handles right into this dashboard.
